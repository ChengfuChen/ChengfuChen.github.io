as both software and hardware engineer in NDT lab of mechanical

We are working on ultrasonic inspection project for AP1000-the safest and most economical nuclear power plant.
We use OpenGL 4.4 to accelerate the display and computation of data. Thanks to lasted GPU technology, our
inspection program performs 20 times faster than traditional CPU program. The red parts showed on the following
pictures are the defects inside the nuclear cell. Our system also has very high resolution. Defect around 0.2mm
can be detected effortlessly.
